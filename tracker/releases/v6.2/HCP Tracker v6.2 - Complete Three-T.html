Step 1: Investment Philosophy & Framework

🎯 Core Beliefs

  • Markets are regime-dependent - Different macro environments require different exposures
  • Diversification across scenarios beats diversification within a single scenario
  • Risk management should focus on avoiding catastrophic outcomes
  • Systematic beats discretionary - Rules-based approach removes emotional bias
  • Probability-weighted optimization captures uncertainty better than point forecasts

🔄 The Four Macro Themes

1. USD Dominance

Tracks the strength and reserve status of the US Dollar through DXY, gold purchases, yuan adoption, and reserve share metrics.

2. AI Productivity Boom

Monitors productivity growth, technology leadership (QQQ/SPY), and corporate profit margins.

3. P/E Mean Reversion

Evaluates valuation metrics including forward P/E, CAPE, and equity risk premium.

4. International Outperformance

Assesses international markets through ACWX/SPY ratio, S&P vs World performance, and capital flows.

🎲 16 Possible Scenarios

The combination of 4 binary themes creates 16 possible market scenarios. The system calculates probabilities for each scenario and optimizes the portfolio accordingly.
